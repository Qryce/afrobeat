The Rise Of AfroBeat

Afrobeat is a style of popular music incorporating elements of African music and jazz, soul, and funk.

A lot of American Pop Stars have been incorporating AfroBeat into their music. From Drake, Chris Brown, R. Kelly, Kanye West, Wale as well as producers such as Major Lazer and Dj Mustard.

Afrobeat has been gaining momentum in the USA. Drake's 'One Dance' topped the Billboard charts just recently. One Dance was heavily influenced by Wizkid, a Nigerian and Dj Maphorisa from South Africa. Major Lazer have also incorporated african smaples in their production. Chris Brown has also worked with Wizkid just recently while Davido scored a Remix of his hit song if with R. Kelly.
